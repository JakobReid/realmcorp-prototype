Our Story

Better
Billing.

We are Realm, a dedicated utility billing service provider based in South East Queensland. Founded in 2023 by David Reid, who brings over 15 years of industry experience, we specialise in water billing for residential buildings and strata communities.

We manage the full billing cycle from start to finish. Our technicians read the meters, validate the data, and produce accurate invoices. We handle payment collection and account reconciliation.

Our Team

Director

David Reid

David commenced his career in the Australian Army, where he honed expertise in electronic systems and automation. In 2006, he moved into embedded utility networks, working on metering, reporting, and efficiency.

Since then, he's worked across the industry with a consistent focus on helping communities manage their resource use. His focus is on getting things right, whether that's regulatory compliance, reducing costs, minimising waste, or improving efficiency.

David is driven by the practical challenge of helping communities use their resources better.

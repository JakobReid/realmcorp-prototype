Frequently Asked Questions

Realm provides billing services for hot water, cold water, electricity, and gas. The specific utilities depend on your building's embedded network setup.
Realm passes on the cost of water or energy as charged to your body corporate or land owner. To view your community's current rates, simply enter your postcode into the search bar on our homepage — your rates will be displayed instantly. Available for existing clients only.
You'll find step-by-step payment instructions in the bottom right corner of your bill. You can also pay directly through the Customer Portal — accessible via the Support Hub or by entering your postcode and selecting your building.
Realm typically bills on the same cycle as your local water authority or energy distributor. The exact frequency will be outlined in your service agreement and shown on your invoice.
If you're unsure or concerned about your current rates, please contact us directly. We'll help identify your water authority or energy distributor and clearly explain how your charges are calculated.
Contact us with your address, lot number, and a photo of your meter. We'll investigate and help resolve the issue as quickly as possible.
Yes. Various government concessions and rebates may apply depending on your circumstances. Contact us or visit the Queensland Government's energy concessions page for the most up-to-date information.
We understand that financial difficulties can arise. If you're experiencing hardship and struggling to pay your bills, please contact us as soon as possible. We'll work with you to find a suitable arrangement.

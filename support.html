Frequently Asked Questions

Realm provides billing services for hot water, cold water, electricity, and gas. The specific utilities depend on your building's embedded network setup.
Realm passes on the cost of water or energy as charged to your body corporate or land owner. To view your community's current rates, simply enter your postcode into the search bar on our homepage and your rates will be displayed instantly. This feature is available to existing clients.
You'll find step-by-step payment instructions in the bottom right corner of your bill. You can also pay directly through the Customer Portal, accessible via the Support Hub or by entering your postcode and selecting your building.
Realm typically bills on the same cycle as your local water authority or energy distributor. The exact frequency will be outlined in your service agreement and shown on your invoice.
If you're unsure or concerned about your current rates, please contact us directly. We'll help identify your water authority or energy distributor and clearly explain how your charges are calculated.
Contact us with your address, lot number, and a photo of your meter. We'll investigate and help resolve the issue as quickly as possible.
We understand that financial difficulties can happen to anyone. If you're struggling to pay your bills, we can help with payment plans, pausing payments, and referrals to support services. See our Financial Hardship section below for full details.
Yes. Depending on your circumstances, you may be eligible for Queensland Government concessions on your utility bills. See our Government Concessions section below for a full list of available schemes.
All of our policies are available as downloadable PDFs. You can find them in our Policy Documents section below, including our Financial Hardship, Domestic Violence Hardship, Complaints, Privacy, and Terms and Conditions policies.

Assistance and Policies

If you're experiencing financial difficulty or want to know about available concessions, we're here to help.

Financial Hardship

Struggling to pay your bill?

Get in touch as early as possible. We'll work with you to find a suitable arrangement so you can stay on top of your account.

  • Flexible payment plans tailored to your situation
  • Temporarily pausing or reducing payments
  • Referrals to financial counselling services
  • No disconnection while you're working with us on a plan
  • All enquiries handled confidentially

Call us on 1300 989 438 or send a message.

Government Concessions

Schemes, rebates and grants you may be eligible for

Depending on your circumstances, government concessions or rebates may apply to your utility bills.

  • Queensland Government Electricity Rebate
  • Reticulated Natural Gas Rebate
  • Home Energy Emergency Assistance Scheme (HEEAS)
  • NSW Rebates and Assistance
  • Medical cooling and heating concessions
  • Eligibility typically requires a Pensioner Concession Card, Health Care Card, or Seniors Card

Not sure what applies to you? Ask us and we'll help you find out.
